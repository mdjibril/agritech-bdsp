**Role-Based Dashboards**, Each role sees a different screen:
- SHF: post harvests, view deals, track payments
- Aggregator: buy from SHFs, manage logistics
- Input Vendor: list inputs, receive orders
- BDSP: create their own mini-network (farmers they onboard), track commissions
- Logistics Partner: accept delivery jobs, confirm POD
- KBS: training hub, certify BDSPs, generate reports
- AGRA: view aggregated data, generate NDPR-compliant reports
- Investor: view opportunities, track portfolio
- V4V Admin: oversee everything, manage escrow

**Core Features**:
- Post and trade commodities (SHF posts harvest → Aggregator buys → Logistics delivers)
- POD confirmation: both trucker and buyer confirm delivery before seller is released
- Auto-commission: system calculates percentages from deals and insurance
- Partner logos (KBS, AGRA) on the marketplace
- Deals, activity log, hubs, network members tracked in the database.
